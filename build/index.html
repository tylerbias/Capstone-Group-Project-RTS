If your castle is destroyed, you lose! Destroy the opponent's castle to win!

Left click to select a unit, or drag to select multiple.

Right click to order selected units to move, attack, or mine gold (peasants only).

Each mining peasant increases your gold per 5 seconds by 2.

Each building periodically creates units, up to 8 units for that building type.

Additional buildings of the same type will increase the maximum possible units of that type by an additional 8.

AVAILABLE BUILDINGS:
(Select a peasant and press key)
T - Tavern - Recruits - 100g
B - Barracks - Knights - 150g
R - Range - Archers - 200g
H - Horse Stables - Cavalry - 250g
